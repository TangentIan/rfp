  1. Project Overview

    Infrastructure South Africa has embarked on a journey to establish itself as a futuristic standalone public entity that will offer itself as a complementary entity within the existing South African infrastructure development and investment ecosystem which contains various existing stakeholders. The purpose of the digital platform is to establish a solid enterprise architecture framework that will ensure the efficient and effective use of the technology stack to be implemented at ISA. The business and technical requirements specifications for all applications and software used at ISA is critically important for establishing and understanding.


    The project is aimed at developing and implementing a Master Digital Platform for ISA by designing and developing an IT Master Digital Platform Operating Model (MDPOM) that combines fourth industrial revolution and digital technologies and operations capabilities in an integrated, well-sequenced way to enable the organisation to achieve its mandate.


  2. Purpose and Scope

    1. Document purpose


      This document outlines business and functional requirements for the Digital Engagement Platform capabilities outlined in the Enterprise architecture that will serve as a basis to define detailed requirements and design the solution.


    2. Document scope


      The scope of this document includes all the current and recommended Digital Engagement Platform business and system requirements that will be used to establish Infrastructure South Africa as a future-proof standalone entity.
